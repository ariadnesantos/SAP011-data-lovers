ABOUT THE SERIES


Walter White is a chemist with a brilliant past as a researcher, but now he's a humble high school teacher in his 50s working in New Mexico. To meet the needs of Skyler, his pregnant wife, and Walt Junior, his physically disabled son, he has to work doubly hard. His life becomes even more complicated when he discovers he's suffering from incurable lung cancer. Walter becomes desperate upon realizing that his family will face hardship after his death and decides he'll do anything to ensure they don't suffer from a lack of money. So, he utilizes his chemistry skills for crime, setting up a drug laboratory to finance his aspirations.
Villain? Good Guy? Opinions are quite divided.
